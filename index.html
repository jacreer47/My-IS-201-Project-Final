My Music Journey

Why I Joined Choir

I joined choir in 6th grade after making a bet with my sister for $20. The bet was that if I joined the choir and hated it after one week, then I would get the $20, however if I enjoyed it after one week I had to commit to it for the rest of school. Well, I accepted the bet and here we are 9 years later. This passion and the many experiences I have had along the way have been absolutely wonderful and I am so glad I joined even if it was because of a bet. I love sharing music and look forward to the many experiences I will continue to have here at BYU with the BYU Choirs. We will be touring Hawaii next year and will have more concerts and trips to come after that.
